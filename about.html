MAKAYLA POLAK

Hey there! My name is MaKayla Polak and I am a proud owner of 28 tattoos. I tattooed myself for the first time when I was 14 and never stopped since. I am a firm believer that your tattoos tell alot about youx. I get tattoos as memorial pieces, whether that be for a lost one or for a hard moment in my life that I want to put to rest, but want to remember my strength through it. I currently live in Missouri, but have gotten tattoos all around the US.

I created this site to help you love your tattoos as much as I do. This is my adivce to you about how to have the best experience with your tattoos, becuase I know how much they mean to you.
