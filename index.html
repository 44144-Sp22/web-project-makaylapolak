Why Do Tattoos Exist?

Tattoos have been around in many different forms for centuries. People get tattoos for a numerous amount of reasons. Whether you are honoring a loved one, fighting a bettle or just adding a piece of art to your body, tattoos are a statement piece.

No matter your reason for getting a tattoo, permenent art on your body is the one thing that you will be able to keep with you for the rest of your life.
